即变为：

2 5 8
3 6 9
>>> ls = [
[1,2,3],
[4,5,6],
[7,8,9]
]
>>> ls
[[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6], [7, 8, 9]]
>>> [y[i] for y in ls for i in range(3)]
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9]
>>> [[y[i]] for y in ls for i in range(3)]
[[1], [2], [3], [4], [5], [6], [7], [8], [9]]
>>> [[y[i] for y in ls] for i in range(3)]

不知道大家看到没，for循环后面打一个","有出奇的作用
zip使用也一定要加 * ，这样就可以颠倒行与列。
来看看zip的使用：
>>> f_name=['A','B','C','E']
>>> l_name=['F','G','H','I']
>>> n_name=['J','K','L','M']
>>> zip(f_name,l_name)
[('A', 'F'), ('B', 'G'), ('C', 'H'), ('E', 'I')]
>>> zip(f_name,l_name,n_name)
[('A', 'F', 'J'), ('B', 'G', 'K'), ('C', 'H', 'L'), ('E', 'I', 'M')]
>>> zip(*zip(f_name,l_name,n_name))
[('A', 'B', 'C', 'E'), ('F', 'G', 'H', 'I'), ('J', 'K', 'L', 'M')]
