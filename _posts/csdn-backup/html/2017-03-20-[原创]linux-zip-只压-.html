linux下，zip命令 只压缩文件 不压缩目录

zhangzhipeng@Earth:data$ ls
weibo
zhangzhipeng@Earth:data$ zip weib.zip -j weibo/result/data.json
  adding: data.json (deflated 77%)

zhangzhipeng@Earth:data$ unzip weib.zip
Archive:  weib.zip
  inflating: data.json

zhangzhipeng@Earth:data$ ls
weib.zip  data.json  weibo
